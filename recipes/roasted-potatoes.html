Roasted Potatoes

Description

These oven roasted potatoes are awesome quick dinner or snack

They melt in your mouth and with the mayo sauce, your guests will likely come back

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Butter
  • Mayonnaise
  • Paprika
  • Brown sugar
  • Vinegar

Steps

  1. Peel, chop potatoes into cubes then boil them for 10 minutes
  2. Preheat oven at 450
  3. Soften 1 stick of butter
  4. Dry the boiled potato cubes and then drizzle with butter
  5. Put in oven for 30-40 minutes turning them half way
