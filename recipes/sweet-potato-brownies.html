Sweet Potato Brownies

Description

These sweet potato brownies are my healthiest to go snack

They are backed with fibers and healthy fats making them non-guilty snack

Ingredients

  • Sweet potatoes (~3)
  • Nut butter (your favorite choice, I go with almond butter)
  • Honey
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Cocoa
  • Flour (I use oat flour)

Steps

  1. Bake sweet potatoes in oven for 45-60 minutes at 450 F
  2. Gently heat the nut butter with 2/3 cups of honey
  3. Mix 6 tablespoonful flour with same amount (6 tbsp) of cocoa
  4. Add 1.5 tsp baking soda and a pinch of salt (1/8 tsp) to the dry mix above
  5. Mix all of the above together and bake in oven at 325F for 20 min.
