Pizza

Description

Home made chewy pizza ready within 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • Pizza flour a.k.a 00 flour: 2 cups
  • Pizza instant yeast: 1 sachet
  • Olive oil: 3 tbsp
  • Low fat milk: 2/3 cup
  • Sugar: 1.5 tsp
  • Salt: 3/4 tsp
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Mix 1 cup of flour with yeast, sugar, salt and olive oil
  2. Heat the low fat milk (don't boil) and add it to the above then mix
  3. Gradually add the remaining 1 cup of flour until the dough is not sticky
  4. Knead for 4 minutes
  5. Roll with wood rolling pin
  6. Add the pizza sauce and cheese plus any toppings of your choice
  7. Heat in the oven for 12-15 minutes at 425 F
